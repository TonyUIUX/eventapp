Shipping and Delivery Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Digital Services

Evorra is a digital platform connecting event organizers and attendees. We provide event listing and discovery services entirely online via our mobile application and website.

2. Delivery of Services

Upon successful payment of the event posting fee, your event listing is immediately activated and published on our platform. The "delivery" of our service is instant and digital.

3. Physical Shipping

Evorra does not sell, ship, or deliver any physical goods. Therefore, standard shipping, tracking, or physical delivery timelines do not apply to our business.

Contact Us

If you experience any delays in your event listing going live after a successful payment, please contact our support team at support@evorra.app.
