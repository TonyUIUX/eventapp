Refund and Cancellation Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Event Posting Fees

The fees charged by Evorra are strictly for the service of listing your event on our platform. Once an event is successfully published, the listing fee is non-refundable.

2. Cancellations by the Organizer

If an organizer decides to cancel their event or delete the listing from the app, no refund will be issued for the posting fee.

3. Rejected Listings

If an event listing violates our terms and is removed by our moderation team, refunds will be evaluated on a case-by-case basis. Fraudulent listings will not receive a refund.

4. Technical Failures

If you are charged but the event fails to list due to a technical error on our platform, please contact our support team within 7 days, and we will process a full refund to your original payment method.

Contact Us

If you need to request a refund due to a technical issue, email us at support@evorra.app.
