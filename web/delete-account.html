Data Deletion Request

If you would like to request the deletion of your account and all associated data, you have two options:

  • Within the app: Go to Profile > Edit Profile > Delete Account.
  • By Email: Contact us at kochigoco@gmail.com with the subject "Data Deletion Request" and include your account email address.

Upon receiving your request, we will delete your account, saved events, and profile information within 14 days.
